Selected Publications

OBS-Diff: Accurate Pruning For Diffusion Models in One-Shot
Junhan Zhu, Hesong Wang, Mingluo Su, Zefang Wang, Huan Wang*
Preprint, 2025 First Author

We propose the first training-free, one-shot pruning framework for diffusion models using a novel timestep-aware Hessian construction, achieving SOTA performance across diverse architectures.
